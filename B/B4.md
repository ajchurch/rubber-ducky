## Lab 02 Thursday 12th March

In this lab activity, we looked at privacy. We got into groups of around 4 or 5 and all had to think of a number without telling anyone what our number was. We then had to find the average of all of our numbers, again, without telling everyone what our number was. My group ended up coming up with the idea of splitting our number up into smaller numbers, and giving one of the smaller numbers to each member. After everyone had given each other a smaller version of our number, we added them up and told everyone our totals which we then averaged out to get our average.

## Lab 03 Thursday 19th March

In this lab activity, we looked at Access Control. My group of 5 had to think of four rules (things we had in common) that no other person in our lab also had (so they could 'breach' access because of our groups rules). 

My group came up with the rules:
1) Must have a keychain
2) Must have an even numbered iPhone model (e.g, iPhone 12, iPhone 14, iPhone 16, etc.)
3) Must play a team sport
4) Must have not travelled overseas in the last 12 months.

We successfully had no one breach all 4 of our rules, and I ended up breaching another groups.
