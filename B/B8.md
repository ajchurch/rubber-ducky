# B8. Participate in a hackathon.

On Saturday 21st March, I attended the Curtin Robotics Club (CRoC) x Red Bull Hackathon (https://croc.cguild.club/public/schedule/events/81406-croc-x-redbull-hackathon) . It was an all day event at Curtin University that had us come up with an idea for an app that incorporated AI in some way to submit to the Red Bull Basement competition.

They encouraged the use of AI to generate a rough vibe and draft of what the idea could look like. My idea was a way to improve the nightlife experience by having a heatmap, live feeds, etc. and then (not implemented) have AI to predict user behaviour to figure out what would be the next best venue to attend.
