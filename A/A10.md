# A10. Discover privacy technique used offline.

A Faraday cage is a physical enclosure that blocks all electromagnetic signals (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cellular, GPS, RFID) from entering or leaving. It was named after scientist Michael Faraday and works by distributing incoming electromagnetic fields across a conductive mesh, effectively cancelling them out inside the enclosure.

My [wallet](https://secrid.com/en-global/wallets/all/) for example, applies the faraday cage principle in its RFID protection to protect my debit cards from being able to be tapped while they're in the special card area.
