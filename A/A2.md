# A2. Discover security concepts used in public space.

## Security Cameras

Probably one of THE most common security concepts used in a public space. A security camera (CCTV) records video in or around properties to monitor activity, enhance safety, and prevent or investigate crime.

## Bollards

These safety bollards are to help prevent cars from accessing pedestrian only sections. They are short vertical posts used primarily to manage traffic, enhance security, and protect pedestrians or property from vehicle collisions

## Security / Bulletproof Glass Windows

Many luxury stores, such as Tiffany and Co in the city store some of their expensive items on the windows, and so there are sensors in the windows in case people try to break the glass to steal the items. This glass is also typically bulletproof or some other kind of secure version so it is significantly harder to break.
