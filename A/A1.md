## Keycards 

Every student (and staff) upon enrolling at UWA is eligible for a UWA card

## UWA Security

UWA Security are actual security people that roam around campus all day and night to ensure the campus is safe for everyone and to be the on call people in case of an incident. 

<img width="326" height="735" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-01 at 4 13 40 pm" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0f725bb3-3af3-4c1e-a644-4b075f9f9c0f" />


## SafeZone App

UWA Security's support is combined alongside UWA's SafeZone app, an app for people to call UWA security, first aid, wellbeing assistance, and emergency services. Users can also report things without needing to call.
