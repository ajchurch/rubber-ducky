## UWA Security & SafeZone App

UWA Security are actual security people that roam around campus all day and night to ensure the campus is safe for everyone and to be the on call people in case of an incident. This is combined alongside UWA's SafeZone app, an app for people to call UWA security, first aid, wellbeing assistance, and emergency services. Users can also report things without needing to call.
