# A29. Find a publicly available AI-generated image, video, or audio clip, use at least one detection or verification tool to analyse it.

After a not very long average reels scroll for me, I discovered this very clearly AI generated video of a dog dancing to MJ https://www.instagram.com/p/DUqL_Efk4rZ/ .

I used an online AI detection tool called [HiveDetect](https://hivedetect.ai/j8QBiZ) . 
<img width="1439" height="830" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-27 at 9 51 44 pm" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f068fc68-e5b5-4e3b-aee4-91fc0978fcf7" />

I downloaded the video and uploaded it to the AI where it detected a 99.9% chance that this content was AI generated. It predicted a 34.6% chance that [https://kling.ai/](KlingAI) was used and a 30.5% chance that Sora2 (a chatgpt ai video model that as of writing this OpenAI have announced they're shutting down) was used. We know for sure that Kling AI was used however though as other AI generated content on this Instagram account contains a Kling AI watermark on the bottom right corner on some videos.
